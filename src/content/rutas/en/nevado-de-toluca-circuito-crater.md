The Nevado de Toluca has something no other mountain near the city offers: you can leave home in the morning and be above 4,000 meters before noon. That closeness is deceptive — the altitude doesn't care that the access was easy.

The crater circuit is short in distance but long in everything else. From the parking area the air already feels different, and as the trail climbs toward Pico del Fraile, the Sol and Luna lagoons stay below as a constant reminder of exactly where you are.

The descent, over the same loose volcanic rock you climbed, is where technique is really tested. It's not a long route, but it's the kind that stays in your legs — and your head — long after you finish it.
